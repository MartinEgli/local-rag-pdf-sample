Helvetia Alpine Sensor Maintenance Runbook

Fictional document created for the Local RAG PDF sample.

Routine Inspection

  • Inspect each field station every 90 days.
  • Replace a battery when reported capacity falls below 20 percent.
  • Record enclosure damage and cable wear before cleaning the station.

Calibration Check

The temperature channel passes the field check when it remains within plus or minus 0.5 degrees Celsius of the reference instrument.

Connectivity Escalation

Escalate to the network operator after three consecutive scheduled uploads are missed. Preserve local buffered readings before restarting a gateway.

Recovery Order

  1. Confirm power and antenna connections.
  2. Record the gateway status code.
  3. Restore connectivity without clearing the local buffer.
  4. Verify that the oldest buffered reading uploads first.

Scope Boundary

This runbook is test data, not operational or safety guidance.
